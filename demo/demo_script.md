# 90-Second Demo Script — "The Eternal Therapist"

## Scene 1: The Problem (0:00–0:15)

**Visual:** Side-by-side comparison
- Left: Generic chatbot. Day 1 → "Hello! How can I help?" Day 2 → "Hello! How can I help?" Day 7 → same thing.
- Right: Text overlay — "AI therapy bots forget you. Every. Single. Time."

**Audio:** "AI therapy bots exist — but they all forget you between sessions. Every conversation starts from zero."

## Scene 2: First Session (0:15–0:30)

**Visual:** Open the app. Type: *"I've been feeling really anxious about my job interview next week."*
- Show the therapist responding with empathy.
- Show the Cognee Cloud `remember()` call happening in the background (styled terminal output).

**Audio:** "The Eternal Therapist remembers. Every word you say is stored in Cognee Cloud's memory graph."

## Scene 3: The Magic — Cross-Session Memory (0:30–0:50)

**Visual:** Fast-forward. Come back "next week."
- Type: *"The interview is tomorrow and I'm panicking."*
- Response: *"I remember your anxiety about this interview. Last week you said you felt underprepared. How has your practice gone?"*
- Show the `recall()` call pulling relevant history.

**Audio:** "Come back anytime. Your therapist remembers your story — the specific job, your specific fear, your specific journey."

## Scene 4: The Knowledge Graph (0:50–1:05)

**Visual:** Switch to the Memory Graph tab.
- Show a radar chart with topic strengths (work: 8, anxiety: 7, relationships: 3...)
- Animate a timelapse of the graph growing as the user has more sessions.

**Audio:** "Under the hood, Cognee Cloud builds a growing knowledge graph of your mental landscape. Topics you discuss get stronger connections. `improve()` enriches the graph after every session."

## Scene 5: Privacy Control (1:05–1:20)

**Visual:** Click "Forget Everything" button.
- Show confirmation.
- Show the graph resetting.
- Show the `forget()` API call.

**Audio:** "And you stay in control. With Cognee Cloud's `forget()`, you can delete specific memories — or wipe your entire history — anytime."

## Scene 6: Close (1:20–1:30)

**Visual:** Logo + tagline.
- "The Eternal Therapist — AI That Never Forgets"
- "Built with Cognee Cloud"
- QR code / link to live demo

**Audio:** "The Eternal Therapist. Because healing shouldn't have a reset button."

---

## Production Notes

- **Screen recording:** Use OBS or built-in screen recorder
- **Background terminal:** Use a styled terminal overlay to show Cognee API calls being made
- **Before/after split:** Use screen split or quick cuts between generic bot and Eternal Therapist
- **Music:** Light, ambient background — nothing distracting
- **Total runtime:** Keep under 90 seconds. Judges watch dozens of demos.
- **End screen:** Hold for 3 seconds with the link visible
